About Nalain Pak

Nalain Pak Acrylic Collection is a premium Islamic decor brand based in Hyderabad, Sindh, Pakistan, founded and owned by Fuzail Ahmed. We specialize in handcrafted acrylic Islamic art that brings divine beauty to your home, car, and workplace.

Every piece is carefully designed and crafted with precision, using high-quality acrylic material that ensures durability and elegance. From car hangings with Ayat-ul-Kursi to beautiful Nalain Pak wall decor, our collection is made to inspire and elevate your spiritual space.

We take pride in serving our customers across Pakistan with authentic Islamic designs, competitive prices, and exceptional customer service.
